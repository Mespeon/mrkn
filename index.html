About

My first foray with programming started way back during the day when overlay layouts for Friendster was the hype. Creating unique layouts using assets I made and adding scripts to make them interactive were more than enough to make me pursue programming further.

I have been a web programmer and a college instructor. When not doing programming, I mentor students, colleagues, and friends who want to learn web development. To date, I have been part of teams that web systems for the Philippine branches of an international restaurant, a cosmetics company, and a finance technology platform and its standalone Checkout service. I was also part of an academic paper that produced a platform for boosting tourism in the province where I live.

Programming aside, I enjoy playing rhythm games, looking for books to read, exploring Teyvat, encountering Raptures, and collecting keyboards.
